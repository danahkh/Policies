Doc Expiry Tracker Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Your document details stay on your device.

1 Overview

Doc Expiry Tracker ("the app") is designed to help you keep track of your document expiry dates in a simple and private way. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

2 No account or registration

  • The app does not require you to create an account.
  • We do not ask you to sign in with email, phone number, or social accounts.

3 Data we collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our own servers.

  • Document details you enter (such as names, dates, notes, and categories) are stored only on your device as part of the app’s normal operation.
  • We do not track your identity, precise location, or contacts.

4 Third‑party ads

To keep the app free, we may display third‑party advertisements. While we do not collect personal information ourselves, ad providers may collect certain technical information from your device in order to show relevant ads.

  • Any data collected by third‑party ad networks is handled under their own privacy policies, not ours.
  • We do not control how those third parties use or share their data.

5 Analytics and tracking

  • We do not use our own analytics tools inside the app.
  • We do not use our own cookies or tracking technologies.

Any tracking performed by third‑party ad networks is governed by their individual privacy policies.

6 Children’s privacy

The app is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Because we do not operate our own user accounts or remote databases, we do not have the ability to identify or manage individual user records.

7 Data retention and deletion

  • All document data you enter is stored locally on your device.
  • Uninstalling the app from your device will remove the app’s local data.

If your device is backed up by your operating system or a cloud service, those backups are controlled by your device or cloud provider, not by us.

8 Security

Because your document information stays on your device, its security depends on how you protect your device (for example, with screen locks, encryption, and backup settings). We do not have access to your personal data and therefore cannot view, modify, or restore it.

9 Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page, and the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates.

10 Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how Doc Expiry Tracker works, please contact us using the contact details provided in the app store listing.
